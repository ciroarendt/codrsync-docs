## FAQ

**Can I use codrsync for free?**
Yes. The Free plan is permanent — full CLI with BYOK, basic envrsync scans, and 1 GB cloud storage.

**What is BYOK?**
Bring Your Own Key. Use your own API keys (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) on any plan. Free plan requires BYOK.

**Do credits expire?**
Credits reset monthly. Pro+ and Teams plans can roll over up to 500 unused credits.

**Can I try Pro before paying?**
Yes. Pro and Pro+ include a 14-day free trial, no credit card required.

**What happens if I exceed storage limits?**
You'll need to upgrade your plan or remove files to stay within limits.
